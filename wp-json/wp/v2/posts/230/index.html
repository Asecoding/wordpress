Metamask Installation.<\/h1>\n<\/header>\n
\n
\n

<\/a>1) Download the Metamask Extenstion<\/h2>\n

Go to\u00a0https:\/\/metamask.io<\/a>\u00a0& click Download in the topright hand corner. Then select your browser type.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

<\/a>2) Installing Metamask Extension.<\/h2>\n

Add Metamask to your Extensions.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

Click Get Started<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

<\/a>3) Obtaining your Metamask Seed Phrase<\/h2>\n

Click on “Create a Wallet” to generate your seedphrase, if you already have one, click Import Wallet.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

If you would like to help Metamask improve their application click \u201cI agree\u201d. MetaMask will never collect any of your private keys, your funds are in your control.
\nIf not, click \u201cNo Thanks\u201d.<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

Insert your password then click “Create”<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

Use this SeedPhrase to recover your Metamask Vault.
\nClick \u201cSave Seed Words as File\u201d and copy the \u201cMetaMask Seed Words\u201d file that is downloaded to a safe place. Click \u201cI\u2019ve Copied It Somewhere Safe\u201d once your seed words file has been secured.<\/p>\n

<\/a>4) Confirming your Metamask Seed Phrase<\/h2>\n

<\/p>\n

After you have written these 12 words down, hit \u201cNext\u201d.
\nCick the words from the list in the exact order that they appeared and they will appear in the clear box above;<\/p>\n

<\/p>\n

Congratulations!
\nThat\u2019s all there is to it. You have now successfully installed MetaMask and set up your first wallet and are pretty much ready to start using the wallet on some dApps.
\nGo ahead and click \u201cAll Done\u201d. This should bring you to your MetaMask wallet;<\/p>\n
