Metamask Installation.

1) Download the Metamask Extenstion

Go to https://metamask.io[1] & click Download in the topright hand corner. Then select your browser type.

2) Installing Metamask Extension.

Add Metamask to your Extensions.

Click Get Started

3) Obtaining your Metamask Seed Phrase

Click on “Create a Wallet” to generate your seedphrase, if you already have one, click Import Wallet.

If you would like to help Metamask improve their application click “I agree”. MetaMask will never collect any of your private keys, your funds are in your control.
If not, click “No Thanks”.

Insert your password then click “Create”

Use this SeedPhrase to recover your Metamask Vault.
Click “Save Seed Words as File” and copy the “MetaMask Seed Words” file that is downloaded to a safe place. Click “I’ve Copied It Somewhere Safe” once your seed words file has been secured.

4) Confirming your Metamask Seed Phrase

After you have written these 12 words down, hit “Next”.
Cick the words from the list in the exact order that they appeared and they will appear in the clear box above;

Congratulations!
That’s all there is to it. You have now successfully installed MetaMask and set up your first wallet and are pretty much ready to start using the wallet on some dApps.
Go ahead and click “All Done”. This should bring you to your MetaMask wallet;
